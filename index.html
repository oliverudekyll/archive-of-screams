Archive of Screams is a project by Lore Isabel Alender, aimed at creating an open, ongoing online archive of political slogan T-shirts spotted around London. Drawing inspiration from the rich history of political T-shirts, the project seeks to shed light on the silent yet powerful ways in which individuals voice their concerns, ideas, and thoughts through wearable statements. By documenting the evolution of political T-shirts from the early 20th century to their resurgence in contemporary times, Archive of Screams aims to amplify marginalized voices and highlight ongoing struggles for social justice and equality within London's vibrant urban landscape.

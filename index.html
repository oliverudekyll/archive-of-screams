initially grew out of a not-so-new
trend

rearising within the London queer club culture.

Kids wearing T-shirts / that were screaming to be heard—

some political, some sarcastic,

some outright
insulting.

While existing within these spaces myself,

I found it valuable to start documenting these garments

and the stories that come with them,

in a typical Gen-Z tradition—

photographing them with my shitty iPhone camera

and posting them initially on my own Instagram.

After a while, a trend emerged,

and I felt the need to make this “archive” public,

at first for my own friends

who all participate in the same subcultural circles,

but also for others,

to people who might never make their way to these spaces,

into this specific energy that these archival images hopefully capture.
